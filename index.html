Introduction

Nashville is the capital of Tennessee. Tennessee is located in the southern region of the United States. There are many things to do in this wonderful city and many things to see! If you are planning to come to Nashville any time soon (please follow COVID-19 guidelines if you are) please plan your trip accordingly as the average tourist pays about $138 per day. This includes hotel, travel, and food expenses, but of course please plan to spend more if you know that you would like something a little extra! Please continue reading to learn more about what you can do in this lively city and more!

What can you do in Nashville?

Great question! Let's find out what you can do! As I stated earlier there are many things to do, but here are 5 fun places/attractions.

  1. Adventureworks Zipline Forest at Fontanel
  2. The Sprocket Rocket Party Bike
  3. The Country Music Hall of Fame and Museum
  4. The Nashville Zoo
  5. The Parthenon in Centennial Park
(This is a picture of the Parthenon in Centennial Park, Nashville, Tennessee. Fun fact: This is the only replica of the one built back in Athens, Greece in the United States! Inside there is also a larger than life statue of the Greek mythical goddess Athena.)
